Waste Management system
This Project aims to organize the collection of Street Waste by setting up trashcans in all public roads of Bhopal. Firstly we will Launch Our Project in Astha in 2022 and after that we will be launched in the rest of the Divisions in the subsequent years.

Ewaste

Electronic waste contains toxic components that are dangerous to human health, such as mercury, lead, cadmium, polybrominated flame retardants, barium and lithium. The negative health effects of these toxins on humans include brain, heart, liver, kidney and skeletal system damage

Plastic

Trashcans will be placed along all Bhopal public roadways as part of this project's effort to systematise the collection of street waste. In 2022, we will start our project in Astha first, and in the years that follow, we will debut it in the remaining Divisions.
